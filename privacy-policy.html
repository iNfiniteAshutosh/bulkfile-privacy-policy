Privacy Policy

BulkFile Downloader Pro — Chrome Extension

Effective date: June 2026

1. What We Collect

BulkFile Downloader Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. We do not track your browsing activity, visited URLs, or downloaded file names.

2. Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The following information is stored only on your own device using chrome.storage.local and never leaves it:

  • Your license key (only to verify Pro status)
  • Your extension settings and preferences
  • Local download history (visible only to you)

This data is never uploaded, shared, or accessible by us or any third party.

3. External Network Requests

The only external request this extension makes is to Gumroad's license verification API when you activate a Pro license key. Only the license key string is sent — no personal data, no device info, no browsing history.

Gumroad's privacy policy can be found at: gumroad.com/privacy

4. Permissions We Use And Why

  • activeTab — To scan the current page for downloadable files
  • downloads — To save files to your device
  • storage — To save your license key and settings locally
  • scripting — To detect files on the active page

We do not request any permissions beyond what is necessary.

5. No Tracking Or Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking or analytics service. No usage data is collected at any point.

6. No Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with any third parties under any circumstances.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes To This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this same URL. Continued use of the extension after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy related questions, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page of BulkFile Downloader Pro.
